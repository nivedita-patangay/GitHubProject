Performance Analytics is a hosted business intelligence offering that transforms data into actionable role-appropriate insights, delivered through the web, mobile or embedded within popular CRM systems. In an increasingly competitive pharmaceutical marketplace, Business intelligence, ETL and analytics are critical to helping you understand and respond to market drivers, optimize your resources, and maximize the value from your investments. To help sales and managed markets teams develop a common set of market insights and make them actionable, IMS Health has partnered with Oracle to develop IMS Performance Analytics, a fully integrated business intelligence service that embeds itself within Siebel CRM and Oracle CRM on Demand.
